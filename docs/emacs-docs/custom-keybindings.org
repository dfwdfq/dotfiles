#+title: Custom keybindings

**~C-x a~ keybinding
*** purpose
move to beginning of buffer.    
    

**~C-x e~ keybinding
*** purpose
move to end of buffer    
    

**~C-q~ keybinding
*** purpose
complete suggestion.    
    

**~M-v v~ keybinding
*** purpose
insert verse in org-mode.    
    

**~M-v c~ keybinding
*** purpose
insert source in org-mode.
