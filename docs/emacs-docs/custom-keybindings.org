#+title: Custom keybindings

** ~C-x a~
*** purpose
move to beginning of buffer.    
    

** ~C-x e~
*** purpose
move to end of buffer    
    

** ~C-q~
*** purpose
complete suggestion.    
    

** ~M-v v~
*** purpose
insert verse in org-mode.    
    

** ~M-v c~
*** purpose
insert source in org-mode.
